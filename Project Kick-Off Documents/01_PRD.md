# SaxStart — Product Requirements Document v1.0

## Overview

**App Name:** SaxStart  
**Platform:** iOS + Android (Flutter)  
**Version:** MVP 1.0  
**Target Launch:** Alto saxophone, absolute beginners, English only

---

## Problem Statement

Beginner saxophone players have no structured, affordable way to practice at home. Existing apps are either too broad (multi-instrument), too expensive (teacher marketplaces), or too passive (YouTube videos). SaxStart gives a complete beginner a daily practice coach in their pocket.

---

## MVP Promise

> "Learn saxophone from your first sound to your first melody — with simple lessons, daily practice, and real-time feedback."

---

## Target Users

**Primary**
- Complete beginners who just bought a saxophone
- Adults learning at home, self-directed
- Teens starting saxophone
- Parents supporting kids who are learning

**Secondary**
- Returning players who need a quick refresher
- School band beginners who want extra practice help

---

## Core MVP Features

### Must Have (v1.0)
1. Onboarding + level selection
2. Lesson path (4 modules, 17 lessons)
3. Fingering chart (beginner notes)
4. Tuner (pitch detection via microphone)
5. Metronome (BPM control)
6. Play Mode — single note scoring (pitch, stability, sustain, attack)
7. Progress tracking (streak, lessons, practice time)

### Not in v1.0
- Live teacher marketplace
- Advanced AI tone coaching
- Full song library
- Multiplayer / community features
- Video upload review
- Sheet music creator
- Tenor / soprano / baritone sax support
- Multiple languages

---

## Navigation Structure

Bottom navigation — 5 tabs:

| Tab | Icon | Purpose |
|-----|------|---------|
| Home | 🏠 | Dashboard, daily task, quick tools |
| Learn | 📖 | Lesson modules and lesson detail |
| Play | ⭐ | Drill modes with real-time scoring |
| Tools | 🎵 | Tuner, metronome, fingering chart |
| Progress | 📊 | Streak, stats, achievements |

## Monetization Plan

**Free Tier**
- Module 1 (all 4 lessons)
- Basic tuner
- Basic metronome
- Fingering chart (beginner notes B, A, G)
- Play Mode: Tune the Note drill

**Premium (SaxStart Pro)**
- All 4 modules + all lessons
- All 3 Play Mode drills
- Full fingering chart
- Structured daily routines
- Detailed score history

Pricing target: $7.99/month or $49.99/year

---

## Success Metrics (MVP)

- Day 7 retention ≥ 30%
- Average session length ≥ 8 minutes
- Play Mode used in ≥ 60% of sessions
- App Store rating ≥ 4.3
